7.4.4.70 Pset_SwitchingDeviceTypeContactor

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcSwitchingDevice / CONTACTOR

Properties

NameTypeDescription
ContactorType P_ENUMERATEDVALUE / IfcLabel / PEnum_ContactorType A list of the available types of contactor from which that required may be selected where: CapacitorSwitching: for switching 3 phase single or multi-step capacitor banks. LowCurrent: requires the use of low resistance contacts. MagneticLatching: enables the contactor to remain in the on position when the coil is no longer energized. MechanicalLatching: requires that the contactor is mechanically retained in the on position. Modular: are totally enclosed and self contained. Reversing: has a double set of contactors that are prewired. Standard: is a generic device that controls the flow of power in a circuit on or off.
