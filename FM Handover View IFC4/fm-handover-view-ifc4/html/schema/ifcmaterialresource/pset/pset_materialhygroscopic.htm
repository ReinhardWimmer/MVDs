8.10.5.6 Pset_MaterialHygroscopic

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcMaterial

Properties

NameTypeDescription
UpperVaporResistanceFactor P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure The vapor permeability relationship of air/material (typically value > 1), measured in high relative humidity (typically in 95/50 % RH).
LowerVaporResistanceFactor P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure The vapor permeability relationship of air/material (typically value > 1), measured in low relative humidity (typically in 0/50 % RH).
IsothermalMoistureCapacity P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcIsothermalMoistureCapacityMeasure Based on water vapor density.
VaporPermeability P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcVaporPermeabilityMeasure The rate of water vapor transmission per unit area per unit of vapor pressure differential under test conditions.
MoistureDiffusivity P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcMoistureDiffusivityMeasure Moisture diffusivity is a transport property that is frequently used in the hygrothermal analysis of building envelope components.
