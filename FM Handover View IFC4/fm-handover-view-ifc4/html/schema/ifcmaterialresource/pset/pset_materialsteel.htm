8.10.5.9 Pset_MaterialSteel

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcMaterial / Steel

Properties

NameTypeDescription
YieldStress P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPressureMeasure A measure of the yield stress (or characteristic 0.2 percent proof stress) of the material.
UltimateStress P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPressureMeasure A measure of the ultimate stress of the material.
UltimateStrain P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure A measure of the (engineering) strain at the state of ultimate stress of the material.
HardeningModule P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcModulusOfElasticityMeasure A measure of the hardening module of the material (slope of stress versus strain curve after yield range).
ProportionalStress P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPressureMeasure A measure of the proportional stress of the material. It describes the stress before the first plastic deformation occurs and is commonly measured at a deformation of 0.01%.
PlasticStrain P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure A measure of the permanent displacement, as in slip or twinning, which remains after the stress has been removed. Currently applied to a strain of 0.2% proportional stress of the material.
Relaxations P_TABLEVALUE / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure Measures of decrease in stress over long time intervals resulting from plastic flow. Different relaxation values for different initial stress levels for a material may be given. It describes the time dependent relative relaxation value for a given initial stress level at constant strain. Relating values are the "RelaxationValue". Related values are the "InitialStress"
