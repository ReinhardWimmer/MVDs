8.15.4.3 Pset_ProfileMechanical

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcProfileDef

Properties

NameTypeDescription
MassPerLength P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcMassPerLengthMeasure Mass per length, i.e. mass of a beam with a unit length of extrusion. For example measured in kg/m.
CrossSectionArea P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcAreaMeasure Area of the profile. For example measured in mm2. If given, the value of the cross section area shall be greater than zero.
Perimeter P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure Perimeter of the profile for calculating the surface area. For example measured in mm.
MinimumPlateThickness P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure This value may be needed for stress analysis and to handle buckling problems. It can also be derived from the given profile geometry or classification and therefore it is only an optional feature allowing for an explicit description. For example measured in mm.
MaximumPlateThickness P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure This value may be needed for stress analysis and to handle buckling problems. It can also be derived from the given profile geometry or classification and therefore it is only an optional feature allowing for an explicit description. For example measured in mm.
CentreOfGravityInX P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLengthMeasure Location of the profile's centre of gravity (geometric centroid), measured along xp.
CentreOfGravityInY P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLengthMeasure Location of the profile's centre of gravity (geometric centroid), measured along yp.
ShearCentreZ P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLengthMeasure Location of the profile's shear centre, measured along zs.
ShearCentreY P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLengthMeasure Location of the profile's shear centre, measured along ys.
MomentOfInertiaY P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcMomentOfInertiaMeasure Moment of inertia about ys (second moment of area, about ys). For example measured in mm4.
MomentOfInertiaZ P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcMomentOfInertiaMeasure Moment of inertia about zs (second moment of area, about zs). For example measured in mm4
MomentOfInertiaYZ P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcMomentOfInertiaMeasure Moment of inertia about ys and zs (product moment of area). For example measured in mm4.
TorsionalConstantX P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcMomentOfInertiaMeasure Torsional constant about xs. For example measured in mm4.
WarpingConstant P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcWarpingConstantMeasure Warping constant of the profile for torsional action. For example measured in mm6.
ShearDeformationAreaZ P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcAreaMeasure Area of the profile for calculating the shear deformation due to a shear force parallel to zs. For example measured in mm². If given, the shear deformation area zs shall be non-negative.
ShearDeformationAreaY P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcAreaMeasure Area of the profile for calculating the shear deformation due to a shear force parallel to ys. For example measured in mm². If given, the shear deformation area ys shall be non-negative.
MaximumSectionModulusY P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcSectionModulusMeasure Bending resistance about the ys axis at the point with maximum zs ordinate. For example measured in mm³.
MinimumSectionModulusY P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcSectionModulusMeasure Bending resistance about the ys axis at the point with minimum zs ordinate. For example measured in mm³.
MaximumSectionModulusZ P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcSectionModulusMeasure Bending resistance about the zs axis at the point with maximum ys ordinate. For example measured in mm³.
MinimumSectionModulusZ P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcSectionModulusMeasure Bending resistance about the zs axis at the point with minimum ys ordinate. For example measured in mm³.
TorsionalSectionModulus P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcSectionModulusMeasure Torsional resistance (about xs). For example measured in mm³.
ShearAreaZ P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcAreaMeasure Area of the profile for calculating the shear stress due to shear force parallel to the section analysis axis zs. For example measured in mm². If given, the shear area zs shall be non-negative.
ShearAreaY P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcAreaMeasure Area of the profile for calculating the shear stress due to shear force parallel to the section analysis axis ys. For example measured in mm². If given, the shear area ys shall be non-negative.
PlasticShapeFactorY P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure Ratio of plastic versus elastic bending moment capacity about the section analysis axis ys. A dimensionless value.
PlasticShapeFactorZ P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure Ratio of plastic versus elastic bending moment capacity about the section analysis axis zs. A dimensionless value.
