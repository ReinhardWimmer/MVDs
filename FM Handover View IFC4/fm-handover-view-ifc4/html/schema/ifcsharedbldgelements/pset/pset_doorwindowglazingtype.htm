6.1.4.12 Pset_DoorWindowGlazingType

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcDoor, IfcWindow

Properties

NameTypeDescription
GlassLayers P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcCountMeasure Number of glass layers within the frame. E.g. "2" for double glazing.
GlassThickness1 P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure Thickness of the first (inner) glass layer.
GlassThickness2 P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure Thickness of the second (intermediate or outer) glass layer.
GlassThickness3 P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure Thickness of the third (outer) glass layer.
FillGas P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLabel Name of the gas by which the gap between two glass layers is filled. It is given for information purposes only.
GlassColor P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLabel Color (tint) selection for this glazing. It is given for information purposes only.
IsTempered P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Indication whether the glass is tempered (TRUE) or not (FALSE) .
IsLaminated P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Indication whether the glass is layered with other materials (TRUE) or not (FALSE).
IsCoated P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Indication whether the glass is coated with a material (TRUE) or not (FALSE).
IsWired P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Indication whether the glass includes a contained wire mesh to prevent break-in (TRUE) or not (FALSE)
VisibleLightReflectance P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure Fraction of the visible light that is reflected by the glazing at normal incidence. It is a value without unit.
VisibleLightTransmittance P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure Fraction of the visible light that passes the glazing at normal incidence. It is a value without unit.
SolarAbsorption P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure (Asol) The ratio of incident solar radiation that is absorbed by a glazing system. It is the sum of the absorption distributed to the exterior (a) and to the interior (qi). Note the following equation Asol + Rsol + Tsol = 1
SolarReflectance P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure (Rsol): The ratio of incident solar radiation that is reflected by a glazing system (also named ρe). Note the following equation Asol + Rsol + Tsol = 1
SolarTransmittance P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure (Tsol): The ratio of incident solar radiation that directly passes through a glazing system (also named τe). Note the following equation Asol + Rsol + Tsol = 1
SolarHeatGainTransmittance P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure (SHGC): The ratio of incident solar radiation that contributes to the heat gain of the interior, it is the solar radiation that directly passes (Tsol or τe) plus the part of the absorbed radiation that is distributed to the interior (qi). The SHGC is refered to also as g-value (g = τe + qi).
ShadingCoefficient P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure (SC): The measure of the ability of a glazing to transmit solar heat, relative to that ability for 3 mm (1/8-inch) clear, double-strength, single glass. Shading coefficient is being phased out in favor of the solar heat gain coefficient (SHGC), and is approximately equal to the SHGC multiplied by 1.15. The shading coefficient is expressed as a number without units between 0 and 1.
ThermalTransmittanceSummer P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcThermalTransmittanceMeasure Thermal transmittance coefficient (U-Value) of a material. Summer thermal transmittance coefficient of the glazing only, often referred to as (U-value).
ThermalTransmittanceWinter P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcThermalTransmittanceMeasure Thermal transmittance coefficient (U-Value) of a material. Winter thermal transmittance coefficient of the glazing only, often referred to as (U-value).
