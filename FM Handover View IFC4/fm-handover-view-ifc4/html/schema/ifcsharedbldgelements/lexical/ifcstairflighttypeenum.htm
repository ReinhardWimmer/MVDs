IfcStairFlightTypeEnum

Semantic definitions at the type

Type definition

This enumeration defines the different types of stair flights that can further specify an IfcStairFlight or IfcStairFlightType.

HISTORY  New enumeration in IFC2x2.

Enumeration definition

ConstantDescription
STRAIGHTA stair flight with a straight walking line.
WINDERA stair flight with a walking line including straight and curved sections.
SPIRALA stair flight with a circular or elliptic walking line.
CURVEDA stair flight with a curved walking line.
FREEFORMA stair flight with a free form walking line (and outer boundaries).
USERDEFINEDUser-defined stair flight.
NOTDEFINEDUndefined stair flight.
