6.1.5.5 Qto_CoveringBaseQuantities

QTO_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcCovering

Quantities

NameTypeDescription
Width Q_LENGTH Nominal width (or thickness) of the plate. Only given, if the covering is prismatic (constant thickess).
GrossArea Q_AREA Sum of all gross areas of the covering facing the space. No opening that is included in the covering is subtracted.
NetArea Q_AREA Sum of all net areas of the covering facing the space. All openings that is included in the covering are subtracted.
