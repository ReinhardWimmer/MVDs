6.1.5.4 Qto_ColumnBaseQuantities

QTO_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcColumn

Quantities

NameTypeDescription
Length Q_LENGTH Total length of the column not taking into account any cut-out's or other processing features.
CrossSectionArea Q_AREA Total area of the cross section (or profile) of the column.
OuterSurfaceArea Q_AREA Total area of the extruded surfaces of the column (not taking into account the end cap areas), normally generated as perimeter * length.
GrossSurfaceArea Q_AREA Total area of the column, normally generated as perimeter * length + 2 * cross section area. It is the sum of OuterSurfaceArea + (2 x CrossSectionArea) and shall only be given, if the OuterSurfaceArea and CrossSectionArea cannot be established separately.
NetSurfaceArea Q_AREA Net surface area of the column, normally generated as perimeter * length + 2 * cross section area taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
GrossVolume Q_VOLUME Total gross volume of the column, not taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
NetVolume Q_VOLUME Total net volume of the column, taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
GrossWeight Q_WEIGHT Total gross weight of the column without add-on parts, not taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
NetWeight Q_WEIGHT Total net weight of the column without add-on parts, taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
