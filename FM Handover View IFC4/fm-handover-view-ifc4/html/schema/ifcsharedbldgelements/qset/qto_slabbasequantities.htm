6.1.5.13 Qto_SlabBaseQuantities

QTO_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcSlab

Quantities

NameTypeDescription
Width Q_LENGTH Nominal width (or thickness) of the slab. Only given, if the slab is prismatic (constant thickess).
Length Q_LENGTH Length (one direction of the non-projected foot print area) of the slab. It shall only be provided, if the slab is rectangular.
Depth Q_LENGTH Depth (one direction of the non-projected foot print area) of the slab. It shall only be provided, if the slab is rectangular.
NOTE  Also refered to as width, but not to be confused with the "Width" quantity, that denotes the thickness in the context of the slab.
Perimeter Q_LENGTH Perimeter measured along the outer boundaries of the slab. Only given, if the slab is prismatic (constant thickness).
GrossArea Q_AREA Total area of the extruded area of the slab. Openings, recesses and projections are not taken into account. Only given, if the slab is prismatic.
NetArea Q_AREA Total area of the extruded area of the slab. Openings and recesses are taken into account by subtraction, projections by addition. Only given, if the slab is prismatic.
GrossVolume Q_VOLUME Total gross volume of the slab. Openings, recesses, and projections are not taken into account.
NetVolume Q_VOLUME Total net volume of the slab. Openings and recesses are taken into account by subtraction, projections by addition.
GrossWeight Q_WEIGHT Total gross weight of the slab without add-on parts, not taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
NetWeight Q_WEIGHT Total net weight of the slab without add-on parts, taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
