6.1.5.9 Qto_PlateBaseQuantities

QTO_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcPlate

Quantities

NameTypeDescription
Width Q_LENGTH Nominal width (or thickness) of the plate. Only given, if the plate is prismatic (constant thickess).
Perimeter Q_LENGTH Perimeter measured along the outer boundaries of the plate. Only given, if the plate is prismatic (constant thickness).
GrossArea Q_AREA Total area of the extruded area of the plate. Openings, recesses and projections are not taken into account. Only given, if the plate is prismatic.
NetArea Q_AREA Total area of the extruded area of the plate. Openings and recesses are taken into account by subtraction, projections by addition. Only given, if the plate is prismatic.
GrossVolume Q_VOLUME Total gross volume of the plate. Openings, recesses, and projections are not taken into account.
NetVolume Q_VOLUME Total net volume of the plate. Openings and recesses are taken into account by subtraction, projections by addition.
GrossWeight Q_WEIGHT Total gross weight of the plate without add-on parts, not taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
NetWeight Q_WEIGHT Total net weight of the plate without add-on parts, taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
