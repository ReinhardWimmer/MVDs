TotalLatentLoad P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure Total energy added or removed from air that affects its humidity or concentration of water vapor. TotalRadiantLoad P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure Total electromagnetic energy added or removed by emission or absorption.
