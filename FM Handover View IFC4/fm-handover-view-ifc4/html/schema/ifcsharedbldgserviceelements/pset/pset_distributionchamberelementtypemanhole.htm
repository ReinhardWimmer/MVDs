6.2.4.6 Pset_DistributionChamberElementTypeManhole

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcDistributionChamberElement / MANHOLE

Properties

NameTypeDescription
InvertLevel P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLengthMeasure Level of the lowest part of the cross section as measured from ground level.
SoffitLevel P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLengthMeasure Level of the highest internal part of the cross section as measured from ground level.
WallMaterial P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcMaterialDefinition The material from which the wall of the chamber is constructed. NOTE: It is assumed that chamber walls will be constructed of a single material.
WallThickness P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure The thickness of the chamber wall construction NOTE: It is assumed that chamber walls will be constructed at a single thickness.
BaseMaterial P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcMaterialDefinition The material from which the base of the chamber is constructed. NOTE: It is assumed that chamber base will be constructed of a single material.
BaseThickness P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure The thickness of the chamber base construction NOTE: It is assumed that chamber base will be constructed at a single thickness.
IsShallow P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Indicates whether the chamber has been designed as being shallow (TRUE) or deep (FALSE).
HasSteps P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Indicates whether the chamber has steps (TRUE) or not (FALSE).
WithBackdrop P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Indicates whether the chamber has a backdrop or tumbling bay (TRUE) or not (FALSE).
AccessCoverMaterial P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcMaterialDefinition The material from which the access cover to the chamber is constructed. NOTE: It is assumed that chamber walls will be constructed of a single material.
AccessLengthOrRadius P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure The length of the chamber access cover or, where the plan shape of the cover is circular, the radius.
AccessWidth P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure The width of the chamber access cover where the plan shape of the cover is not circular.
AccessCoverLoadRating P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcText The load rating of the access cover (which may be a value or an alphanumerically defined class rating).
