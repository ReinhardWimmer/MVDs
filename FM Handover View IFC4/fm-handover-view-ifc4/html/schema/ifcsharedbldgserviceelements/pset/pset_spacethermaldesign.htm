6.2.4.24 Pset_SpaceThermalDesign

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcSpace

Properties

NameTypeDescription
CoolingDesignAirflow P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcVolumetricFlowRateMeasure The air flowrate required during the peak cooling conditions.
HeatingDesignAirflow P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcVolumetricFlowRateMeasure The air flowrate required during the peak heating conditions, but could also be determined by minimum ventilation requirement or minimum air change requirements.
TotalSensibleHeatGain P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPowerMeasure The total sensible heat or energy gained by the space during the peak cooling conditions.
TotalHeatGain P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPowerMeasure The total amount of heat or energy gained by the space at the time of the space's peak cooling conditions.
TotalHeatLoss P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPowerMeasure The total amount of heat or energy lost by the space at the time of the space's peak heating conditions.
CoolingDryBulb P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcThermodynamicTemperatureMeasure Inside dry bulb temperature for cooling design.
CoolingRelativeHumidity P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure Inside relative humidity for cooling design.
HeatingDryBulb P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcThermodynamicTemperatureMeasure Inside dry bulb temperature for heating design.
HeatingRelativeHumidity P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure Inside relative humidity for heating design.
VentilationAirFlowrate P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcVolumetricFlowRateMeasure Ventilation outside air requirement for the space.
ExhaustAirFlowrate P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcVolumetricFlowRateMeasure Design exhaust air flow rate for the space.
CeilingRAPlenum P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Ceiling plenum used for return air or not. TRUE = Yes, FALSE = No.
BoundaryAreaHeatLoss P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcHeatFluxDensityMeasure Heat loss per unit area for the boundary object. This is a design input value for use in the absence of calculated load data.
