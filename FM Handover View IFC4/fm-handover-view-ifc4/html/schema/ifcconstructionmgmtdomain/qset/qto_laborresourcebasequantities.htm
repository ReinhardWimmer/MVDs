7.3.5.3 Qto_LaborResourceBaseQuantities

QTO_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcLaborResource

Quantities

NameTypeDescription
StandardWork Q_TIME Work that is performed at regular times, up to a particular limit after which overtime rates may apply.
OvertimeWork Q_TIME Work that is performed after exceeding a particular limit such as hours per day and/or hours per week, after which company or municipal policy requires a different rate to apply. Note: Policies for when overtime takes effect are the responsibility of the user or application; they are not modelled in IFC.
