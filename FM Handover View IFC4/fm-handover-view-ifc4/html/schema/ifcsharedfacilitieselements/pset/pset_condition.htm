6.4.4.2 Pset_Condition

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcElement

Properties

NameTypeDescription
AssessmentDate P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcDate Date on which the overall condition is assessed
AssessmentCondition P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLabel The overall condition of a product based on an assessment of the contributions to the overall condition made by the various criteria considered. The meanings given to the values of assessed condition should be agreed and documented by local agreements. For instance, is overall condition measured on a scale of 1 - 10 or by assigning names such as Good, OK, Poor.
AssessmentDescription P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcText Qualitative description of the condition.
