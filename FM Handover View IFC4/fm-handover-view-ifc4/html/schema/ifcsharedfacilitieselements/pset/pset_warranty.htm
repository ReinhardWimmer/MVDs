      WarrantyPeriodMODIFIEDType changed from IfcTimeMeasure to IfcDuration.
      PointOfContactMODIFIEDInstantiation changed from P_REFERENCEVALUE to P_SINGLEVALUE.

Properties

NameTypeDescription
WarrantyIdentifier P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcIdentifier The identifier assigned to a warranty.
WarrantyStartDate P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcDate The date on which the warranty commences.
WarrantyEndDate P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcDate The date on which the warranty expires.
IsExtendedWarranty P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Indication of whether this is an extended warranty whose duration is greater than that normally assigned to an artefact (=TRUE) or not (= FALSE).
WarrantyPeriod P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcDuration The time duration during which a manufacturer or supplier guarantees or warrants the performance of an artefact.
WarrantyContent P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcText The content of the warranty.
PointOfContact P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLabel The organization that should be contacted for action under the terms of the warranty. Note that the role of the organization (manufacturer, supplier, installer etc.) is determined by the IfcActorRole attribute of IfcOrganization.
Exclusions P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcText Items, conditions or actions that may be excluded from the warranty or that may cause the warranty to become void.
