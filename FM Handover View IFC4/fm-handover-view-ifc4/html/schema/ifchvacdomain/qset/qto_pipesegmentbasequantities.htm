7.5.5.24 Qto_PipeSegmentBaseQuantities

QTO_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcPipeSegment

Quantities

NameTypeDescription
Length Q_LENGTH Length of the segment, calculated at midpoint of cross-section, equal to the distance between inlet and outlet ports.
GrossCrossSectionArea Q_AREA Area of the cross section, including the pipe itself and the interior flow space.
NetCrossSectionArea Q_AREA Area of the cross section of the pipe, excluding the interior flow space.
OuterSurfaceArea Q_AREA Total area of the extruded surfaces of the pipe (not taking into account the end cap areas), normally generated as perimeter * length.
GrossWeight Q_WEIGHT Weight of the pipe segment itself, not including contained fluid.
NetWeight Q_WEIGHT Weight of the pipe segment, including contained fluid as designed.
