7.5.5.14 Qto_DuctSegmentBaseQuantities

QTO_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcDuctSegment

Quantities

NameTypeDescription
Length Q_LENGTH Length of the segment, calculated at midpoint of cross-section, equal to the distance between inlet and outlet ports.
GrossCrossSectionArea Q_AREA Area of the cross section, including the duct itself and the interior flow space.
NetCrossSectionArea Q_AREA Area of the cross section of the duct, excluding the interior flow space.
OuterSurfaceArea Q_AREA Total area of the extruded surfaces of the duct (not taking into account the end cap areas), normally generated as perimeter * length.
GrossWeight Q_WEIGHT Weight of the duct segment.
