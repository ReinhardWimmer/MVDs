Properties

NameTypeDescription
AirFlowRate P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcVolumetricFlowRateMeasure Volumetric flow rate.
NeckAirVelocity P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcLinearVelocityMeasure Air velocity at the neck.
SupplyAirTemperatureHeating P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcThermodynamicTemperatureMeasure Supply air temperature in heating mode.

PressureDrop P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPressureMeasure Drop in total pressure between inlet and outlet at nominal air-flow rate.
InductionRatio P_TABLEVALUE / IfcReal / IfcLengthMeasure Induction ratio versus distance from the diffuser and its discharge direction; induction ratio (or entrainment ratio) is the ratio of the volumetric flow rate in the jet to the volumetric flow rate at the air terminal.
CenterlineAirVelocity P_TABLEVALUE / IfcLinearVelocityMeasure / IfcLengthMeasure Centerline air velocity versus distance from the diffuser and temperature differential; a function of distance from diffuser and temperature difference between supply air and room air.
