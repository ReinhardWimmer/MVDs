7.5.4.50 Pset_FanOccurrence

PSET_OCCURRENCEDRIVEN / IfcFan

Properties

NameTypeDescription
DischargeType P_ENUMERATEDVALUE / IfcLabel / PEnum_FanDischargeType Defines the type of connection at the fan discharge. Duct: Discharge into ductwork. Screen: Discharge into screen outlet. Louver: Discharge into a louver. Damper: Discharge into a damper.
ApplicationOfFan P_ENUMERATEDVALUE / IfcLabel / PEnum_FanApplicationType The functional application of the fan. SupplyAir: Supply air fan. ReturnAir: Return air fan. ExhaustAir: Exhaust air fan. Other: Other type of application not defined above.
CoilPosition P_ENUMERATEDVALUE / IfcLabel / PEnum_FanCoilPosition Defines the relationship between a fan and a coil. DrawThrough: Fan located downstream of the coil. BlowThrough: Fan located upstream of the coil.
MotorPosition P_ENUMERATEDVALUE / IfcLabel / PEnum_FanMotorPosition Defines the location of the motor relative to the air stream. InAirStream: Fan motor is in the air stream. OutOfAirStream: Fan motor is out of the air stream.
FanMountingType P_ENUMERATEDVALUE / IfcLabel / PEnum_FanMountingType Defines the method of mounting the fan in the building.
FractionOfMotorHeatToAirStream P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure Fraction of the motor heat released into the fluid flow.
ImpellerDiameter P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure Diameter of fan wheel - used to scale performance of geometrically similar fans.
