7.5.4.19 Pset_CompressorPHistory

PSET_PERFORMANCEDRIVEN / IfcCompressor

Properties

NameTypeDescription
CompressorCapacity P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure The product of the ideal capacity and the overall volumetric efficiency of the compressor.
EnergyEfficiencyRatio P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcReal Energy efficiency ratio (EER).
CoefficientOfPerformance P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure Coefficient of performance (COP).
VolumetricEfficiency P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure Ratio of the actual volume of gas entering the compressor to the theoretical displacement of the compressor.
CompressionEfficiency P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure Ratio of the work required for isentropic compression of the gas to the work delivered to the gas within the compression volume (as obtained by measurement).
MechanicalEfficiency P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure Ratio of the work (as measured) delivered to the gas to the work input to the compressor shaft.
IsentropicEfficiency P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure Ratio of the work required for isentropic compression of the gas to work input to the compressor shaft.
CompressorTotalEfficiency P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure Ratio of the thermal cooling capacity to electrical input.
ShaftPower P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure The actual shaft power input to the compressor.
InputPower P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure Input power to the compressor motor.
LubricantPumpHeatGain P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure Lubricant pump heat gain.
FrictionHeatGain P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure Friction heat gain.
CompressorTotalHeatGain P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure Compressor total heat gain.
FullLoadRatio P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcNormalisedRatioMeasure Ratio of actual power to full load power as a quadratic function of part load, at certain condensing and evaporating temperature, FracFullLoadPower = f ( PartLoadRatio).
