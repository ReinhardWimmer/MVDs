7.5.4.23 Pset_CooledBeamPHistory

PSET_PERFORMANCEDRIVEN / IfcCooledBeam

Properties

NameTypeDescription
TotalCoolingCapacity P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure Total cooling capacity. This includes cooling capacity of beam and cooling capacity of supply air.
TotalHeatingCapacity P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure Total heating capacity. This includes heating capacity of beam and heating capacity of supply air.
BeamCoolingCapacity P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure Cooling capacity of beam. This excludes cooling capacity of supply air.
BeamHeatingCapacity P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPowerMeasure Heating capacity of beam. This excludes heating capacity of supply air.
CoolingWaterFlowRate P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcVolumetricFlowRateMeasure Water flow rate for cooling.
HeatingWaterFlowRate P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcVolumetricFlowRateMeasure Water flow rate for heating.
CorrectionFactorForCooling P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcReal Correction factor k as a function of water flow rate (used to calculate cooling capacity).
CorrectionFactorForHeating P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcReal Correction factor k as a function of water flow rate (used to calculate heating capacity).
WaterPressureDropCurves P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcPressureMeasure Water pressure drop as function of water flow rate.
SupplyWaterTemperatureCooling P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcThermodynamicTemperatureMeasure Supply water temperature in cooling mode.
ReturnWaterTemperatureCooling P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcThermodynamicTemperatureMeasure Return water temperature in cooling mode.
SupplyWaterTemperatureHeating P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcThermodynamicTemperatureMeasure Supply water temperature in heating mode.
ReturnWaterTemperatureHeating P_REFERENCEVALUE / IfcTimeSeries / IfcThermodynamicTemperatureMeasure Return water temperature in heating mode.
