Properties

NameTypeDescription

OperationPressureMax P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPressureMeasure Maximum pressure under normal operating conditions.
ParticleAbsorptionCurve P_TABLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure / IfcPositiveRatioMeasure Ratio of particles that are removed by the filter. For example, given for 3 significant particle sizes >= 0,1 micro m, >= 1 micro m, >= 5 micro m
AutomaticCondensateDischarge P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Whether or not the condensing water or oil is discharged automatically from the filter.
