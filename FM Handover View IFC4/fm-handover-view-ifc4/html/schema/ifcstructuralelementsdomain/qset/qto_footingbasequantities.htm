7.8.5.1 Qto_FootingBaseQuantities

QTO_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcFooting

Quantities

NameTypeDescription
Length Q_LENGTH Length of the footing, not taking into account any cut-out's or other processing features. For strip footings it is measured along the path, for other footings it is one of the horizontal dimensions. It should only be provided, if it is constant.
Width Q_LENGTH Total nominal width (or thickness) of the footing. For strip footings it is measured perpendicular to the footing path (or longitudial axis). For other footings it is one of the horizontal dimensions. It should only be provided, if it is constant.
Height Q_LENGTH Total nominal height of the footing. It should only be provided, if it is constant.
CrossSectionArea Q_AREA Total area of the cross section (or profile) of the footing.
OuterSurfaceArea Q_AREA Total area of the extruded surfaces of the footing (not taking into account the end cap areas), normally generated as perimeter * length.
GrossSurfaceArea Q_AREA Total area of the footing, normally generated as perimeter * length + 2 * cross section area. It is the sum of OuterSurfaceArea + (2 x CrossSectionArea) and shall only be given, if the OuterSurfaceArea and CrossSectionArea cannot be established separately.
GrossVolume Q_VOLUME Total gross volume of the footing, not taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
NetVolume Q_VOLUME Total net volume of the footing, taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
GrossWeight Q_WEIGHT Total gross weight of the footing without add-on parts, not taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
NetWeight Q_WEIGHT Total net weight of the footing without add-on parts, taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
