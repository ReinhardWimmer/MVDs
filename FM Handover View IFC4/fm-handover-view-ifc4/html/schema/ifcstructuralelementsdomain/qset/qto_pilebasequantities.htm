7.8.5.2 Qto_PileBaseQuantities

QTO_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcPile

Quantities

NameTypeDescription
Length Q_LENGTH Total length of the pile not taking into account any cut-out's or other processing features.
CrossSectionArea Q_AREA Total area of the cross section (or profile) of the pile.
OuterSurfaceArea Q_AREA Total area of the extruded surfaces of the pile (not taking into account the end cap areas), normally generated as perimeter * length.
GrossSurfaceArea Q_AREA Total area of the pile, normally generated as perimeter * length + 2 * cross section area. It is the sum of OuterSurfaceArea + (2 x CrossSectionArea) and shall only be given, if the OuterSurfaceArea and CrossSectionArea cannot be established separately.
GrossVolume Q_VOLUME Total gross volume of the pile, not taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
NetVolume Q_VOLUME Total net volume of the pile, taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
GrossWeight Q_WEIGHT Total gross weight of the pile without add-on parts, not taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
NetWeight Q_WEIGHT Total net weight of the pile without add-on parts, taking into account possible processing features (cut-out's, etc.) or openings and recesses.
