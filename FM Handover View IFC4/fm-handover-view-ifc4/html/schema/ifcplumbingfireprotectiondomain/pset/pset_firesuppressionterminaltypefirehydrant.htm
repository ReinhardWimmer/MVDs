7.6.4.3 Pset_FireSuppressionTerminalTypeFireHydrant

PSET_TYPEDRIVENOVERRIDE / IfcFireSuppressionTerminal / FIREHYDRANT

Properties

NameTypeDescription
FireHydrantType P_ENUMERATEDVALUE / IfcLabel / PEnum_FireHydrantType Defines the range of hydrant types from which the required type can be selected where. DryBarrel: A hydrant that has isolating valves fitted below ground and that may be used where the possibility of water freezing is a consideration. WetBarrel: A hydrant that has isolating valves fitted above ground and that may be used where there is no possibility of water freezing.
PumperConnectionSize P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure The size of a connection to which a fire hose may be connected that is then linked to a pumping unit.
NumberOfHoseConnections P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcInteger The number of hose connections on the hydrant (excluding the pumper connection).
HoseConnectionSize P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure The size of connections to which a hose may be connected (other than that to be linked to a pumping unit).
DischargeFlowRate P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcVolumetricFlowRateMeasure The volumetric rate of fluid discharge.
FlowClass P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLabel Alphanumeric indication of the flow class of a hydrant (may be used in connection with or instead of the FlowRate property).
WaterIsPotable P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcBoolean Indication of whether the water flow from the hydrant is potable (set TRUE) or non potable (set FALSE).
PressureRating P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPressureMeasure Maximum pressure that the hydrant is manufactured to withstand.
BodyColor P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcText Color of the body of the hydrant. Note: Consult local fire regulations for statutory colors that may be required for hydrant bodies in particular circumstances.
CapColor P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcText Color of the caps of the hydrant. Note: Consult local fire regulations for statutory colors that may be required for hydrant caps in particular circumstances.
