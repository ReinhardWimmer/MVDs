Properties

NameTypeDescription

InletConnectionSize P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure Size of the inlet connection to the hose reel.
HoseDiameter P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure Notional diameter (bore) of the hose.
HoseLength P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcPositiveLengthMeasure Notional length of the hose fitted to the hose reel when fully extended.
HoseNozzleType P_ENUMERATEDVALUE / IfcLabel / PEnum_HoseNozzleType Identifies the predefined types of nozzle (in terms of spray pattern) fitted to the end of the hose from which the type required may be set.
ClassOfService P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLabel A classification of usage of the hose reel that may be applied.
ClassificationAuthority P_SINGLEVALUE / IfcLabel The name of the authority that applies the classification of service to the hose reel (e.g. NFPA/FEMA).
