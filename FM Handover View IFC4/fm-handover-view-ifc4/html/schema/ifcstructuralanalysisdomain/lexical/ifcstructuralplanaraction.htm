Semantic definitions at the entity

Entity definition

This entity defines an action with constant value which is distributed over a surface.

HISTORY  New entity in IFC2x2.
IFC4 CHANGE  Intermediate supertype IfcStructuralSurfaceAction inserted.
NOTE  Like its supertype IfcStructuralSurfaceAction, this action type may also act on curved faces.

Formal Propositions

RuleDescription
SuitableLoadTypeA planar action shall place either a planar force or a temperature load.
ConstPredefinedTypeThis surface action subtype is restricted to constant load distribution over its domain.
